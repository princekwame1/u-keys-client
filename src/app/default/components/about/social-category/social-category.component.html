How to create a Social Media Category
  • Click on the addition button in the Social Media Category table.
  • Enter the Social media icon code. For example :
    icons icon-code
    fa fa-facebook-official
    fa fa-telegram
    fa fa-linkedin
    fa fa-instagram
    fa fa-whatsapp

    • Icons are supported by Font Awesome. Visit their Official Website for more information
